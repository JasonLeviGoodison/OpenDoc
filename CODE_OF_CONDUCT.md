# Code of Conduct

OpenDoc aims to be a respectful, technically serious open-source project.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume good faith, but back technical claims with evidence.
- Keep discussions focused on the code, product, and user impact.
- Welcome contributors with different backgrounds and experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or personal attacks
- Discriminatory or hateful language
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- Repeated bad-faith disruption of issues, discussions, or pull requests

## Maintainer Responsibilities

Project maintainers may edit, remove, or lock content that violates this code of conduct. They may also restrict participation when needed to keep the project safe and productive.
